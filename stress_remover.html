Stress Remover Exercises

1. Deep Breathing

Instructions: Sit comfortably with your back straight. Inhale deeply through your nose, filling your lungs with air. Exhale slowly through your mouth. Repeat for several minutes.

2. Shoulder Roll

Instructions: Relax your shoulders. Roll your shoulders backward in a circular motion, then forward. Repeat several times to release tension.

3. Neck Stretch

Instructions: Gently tilt your head to one side, feeling the stretch in your neck. Hold for 15-30 seconds, then switch sides.

4. Mindful Walking

Instructions: Take a slow, mindful walk. Pay attention to each step, the sensation of your feet touching the ground, and your surroundings. Focus on the present moment.

5. Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Instructions: Starting from your toes, tense each muscle group for a few seconds, then release. Gradually work your way up through your body, tensing and relaxing each muscle group.
