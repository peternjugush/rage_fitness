AND ALSO SOME NICE TUTORIALS FOR YOU ;)

Exercise 1: Leg Swings

1.Hold onto a stable surface for balance. 2.Swing one leg forward and backward in a controlled motion. 3.Repeat for 10 reps on each leg.

Exercise 2: Shoulder Rolls

Stand tall with your arms hanging by your sides. Roll your shoulders backwards in a circular motion. Repeat for 10 reps.

3. Hip Flexor Stretch

Instructions: Step one foot forward into a lunge position. Lean forward, keeping your back straight, to stretch the hip flexor of the back leg. Hold for 15-30 seconds, then switch legs.

4. Neck Stretch

Instructions: Gently tilt your head to one side, feeling the stretch in your neck. Hold for 15-30 seconds, then switch sides.
